Data collection, management, usage, and sharing

Currently, we collect user information from users such as parents. The information involving both basic user registration information and user sensitive information as described from the following list:

Basic information

we collect information from you when you register to our app or enter information with our app. This includes your first name, surname, email address, your mobile phone number, and your current location. Regarding your child information, it consists of the your child name, name of school that your child attending as well as classroom number.Regarding the geographic information locations, we use Google Map API and store location information on secure cloud server. We obtain your geographic data in order to display your location on your mobile devices, and the rest in the process of data management including registration, search, etc. Moreover, the app will also retrieve the geographic information form your mobile device during some period from the usage of the application
